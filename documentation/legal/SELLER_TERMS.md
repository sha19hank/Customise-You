## 6. Payment and Payouts

### 6.1 Payment Collection

- All customer payments are collected by **CustomiseYou** through secure payment gateways
- You do not collect payments directly from customers
- Cash on Delivery (COD) is **not currently supported** on the platform

### 6.2 Platform Commission

CustomiseYou charges a **platform commission** on each completed order. The commission structure is:

- **First 10 orders:** 0% commission (promotional period)
- **Orders 11-50:** 3% commission
- **Orders 51 and above:** 7% commission

Commission is deducted from the order total before payout.

### 6.3 Payout Schedule

- Payouts are processed to your registered bank account
- Payout frequency and schedule are determined by CustomiseYou
- You must provide accurate bank account details to receive payouts
- **Bank account verification is required** for payout eligibility

### 6.4 Taxes and Deductions

- You are responsible for all taxes on your earnings
